About the club

We combine coding and hardware to solve problems. Members work in teams and present builds at the end of each month.

Mission

Grow practical skills in software, robotics and teamwork through projects.

Culture

Respect, consistency, sharing knowledge, and building before overthinking.

For beginners

No experience required. We start from basics and pair students up.
